Goal: To lose weight gradually and maintain a balanced diet.

Meal Plan:

Breakfast: Whole grain cereal with skim milk and sliced bananas.
Mid-Morning Snack: A piece of fruit (e.g., an orange or an apple)
Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, and balsamic vinaigrette.
Afternoon Snack: Air-popped popcorn (unsalted).
Dinner: Baked cod with quinoa and steamed broccoli.
Before Bed: A small bowl of non-fat Greek yogurt.
