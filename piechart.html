Goal: To lose weight steadily while maintaining proper nutrition.

Meal Plan:

Breakfast: Vegetable omelette with whole grain toast and a side of berries.
Mid-Morning Snack: Celery sticks with hummus.
Lunch: Grilled tofu with brown rice and stir-fried vegetables.
Afternoon Snack: Low-fat cottage cheese with cherry tomatoes.
Dinner: Turkey meatballs with whole wheat spaghetti and marinara sauce, steamed green beans.
Before Bed: Herbal tea or a small handful of almonds.
